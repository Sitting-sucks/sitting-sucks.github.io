Refund Policy

14-Day Money-Back Guarantee. If you're not happy with a digital product or service purchase, email us within 14 days of purchase and we'll refund you in full. No hoops, no hard feelings.

We want you to be confident in what you buy. Our refund terms by product type:

  • Digital products (The Complete Claude Guide, AI Skills Packs, templates): full refund within 14 days of purchase.
  • The Hermes Stack: full refund within 14 days of purchase if the installer doesn't work for you or you're not satisfied.
  • Website builds & Local AI Command Center installs: custom service work is refundable for any amount not yet delivered. If we've already started, we'll refund the portion of work not completed. Talk to us — we'll be fair.

How to request a refund

Email ryansaisetup@gmail.com with your order/receipt details and a one-line reason (optional, but it helps us improve). Refunds are issued to your original payment method via Stripe, typically within 5–10 business days.

Privacy Policy

We keep data collection to the minimum needed to deliver products and answer your questions. We do not sell your personal information. Ever.

What we collect

  • Email address — when you join a waitlist, email us, or buy a product, so we can deliver your purchase and respond to you.
  • Booking & consultation details — when you book a call through Cal.com, they collect your name, email, and scheduling info to set up the meeting. See Cal.com's own privacy policy for how they handle that data.
  • Payment information — handled entirely by Stripe. We never see or store your full card details.

How we use it

  • To deliver the products and services you purchase.
  • To respond to your questions and support requests.
  • To let you know when a waitlisted product launches (only if you opted in).

Who we share it with

Only the service providers we need to operate: Stripe (payments), Cal.com (booking), and our email provider. We don't sell, rent, or trade your data to anyone.

Your choices

You can ask us to delete your data, unsubscribe from emails, or get a copy of what we hold on you at any time — just email ryansaisetup@gmail.com.

Contact

Questions about either policy? Email ryansaisetup@gmail.com and a real human (helped by an AI teammate) will get back to you.
