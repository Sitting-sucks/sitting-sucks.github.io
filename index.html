Frequently Asked Questions

Just straight answers.

Do I need to be technical?
Not at all. I handle everything technical. If you can type a message, you can use your AI. Think of it like texting a very smart coworker.
What's the difference between the three services?
Claude AI Setup ($399) gets you a personal AI assistant on your computer — it's the fastest way to start using AI. Add tool connections (email, calendar, etc.) for a flat $150. Online Brand & Website (from $1,500) is your complete online presence — I design and build a professional website, set up payments, handle hosting, and get you ready to sell online. Local AI Command Center ($2,500–$25,000+) is a dedicated machine running AI models that never send data to the internet — for individuals or entire businesses. I also offer custom automation workflows and other add-ons that pair with any service. We'll figure out the right fit during your free call.
What does my AI subscription cost?
For the Claude AI Setup and Automation Suite, you'll need a Claude Pro subscription (~$20/month), paid directly to Anthropic. My setup fee is separate and one-time. For the Local AI Command Center, the AI runs entirely on your machine — no subscription needed. I explain all of this during our call.
What if I don't like it?
14-day money-back guarantee on the Claude AI Setup and Automation Suite. For the Command Center, return the hardware in original condition for a full refund. No questions asked.
Is my data safe?
With the Claude setup, your files stay on your computer — only the specific text you send to Claude goes to their servers (same as any AI tool). With the Local AI Command Center, nothing ever leaves your machine. Period. That's why it's the choice for law firms, healthcare practices, and anyone handling sensitive data.
What tools can you connect?
Gmail, Outlook, Google Calendar, LinkedIn, Instagram, X, Notion, Slack, Stripe, Google Drive, and many more. If it has a way to connect, I can probably hook it up. We'll figure out what you need during our call.
What kind of hardware do I need for the Local AI?
I recommend either a Mac Mini or Mac Studio depending on your needs and budget. The Mac Mini M4 Pro with 64GB (~$2,099) is the sweet spot for most businesses — it runs powerful AI models, it's silent, compact, and energy-efficient. I help you choose the right machine and handle all the setup.
Can we do this in person?
Yes! I offer in-person setup for local clients. Everything can also be done remotely via screenshare if that's easier.
How do I pay?
I accept Stripe (card payments) and Venmo. Payment is due at the start of the setup session, after our free consultation. The initial call is always free.
Do you have a referral program?
Yes! Refer someone to any of my services and when they sign up and pay, you get $100 back. No limit on how many people you can refer. Just have them mention your name when they book their call.
