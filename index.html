Frequently Asked Questions

Just straight answers.

Do I need to be technical?
Not at all. I handle everything technical. If you can type a message, you can use your AI. Think of it like texting a very smart coworker.
What's the difference between the products?
The Complete Claude Guide ($29) is the fastest way in — an instant PDF that teaches you to get real work out of Claude. The Hermes Stack ($1,497) is the flagship: your complete AI teammate, installed and configured in about 10 minutes, totally self-serve. Website Builds (from $1,500) are custom sites built for you on free hosting — you own everything. Local AI Command Center ($2,500–$25,000+) is a dedicated machine running AI models that never touch the cloud — for individuals or entire businesses. The Guide and Hermes Stack are buy-and-go; the custom builds start with one short scoping call.
Are there any ongoing fees?
Nothing to me — every product is one-time. The Hermes Stack uses Claude, so you'll want a Claude plan (the free tier works to start; Pro is ~$20/month paid directly to Anthropic). The Local AI Command Center runs entirely on your own machine, so it needs no subscription at all. Website Builds are hosted free on GitHub Pages — no monthly hosting bill.
What if I don't like it?
14-day money-back guarantee on the digital products. For the Command Center, return the hardware in original condition for a full refund. No questions asked.
Is my data safe?
With the Hermes Stack, your files stay on your computer — only the specific text you send to Claude goes to their servers (same as any AI tool). With the Local AI Command Center, nothing ever leaves your machine. Period. That's why it's the choice for law firms, healthcare practices, and anyone handling sensitive data.
What tools can you connect?
Gmail, Outlook, Google Calendar, LinkedIn, Instagram, X, Notion, Slack, Stripe, Google Drive, and many more. If it has a way to connect, I can probably hook it up. We'll figure out what you need during our call.
What kind of hardware do I need for the Local AI?
I recommend either a Mac Mini or Mac Studio depending on your needs and budget. The Mac Mini M4 Pro with 64GB (~$2,099) is the sweet spot for most businesses — it runs powerful AI models, it's silent, compact, and energy-efficient. I help you choose the right machine and handle all the setup.
Can we do this in person?
Yes! I offer in-person setup for local clients. Everything can also be done remotely via screenshare if that's easier.
How do I pay?
Digital products (the Guide and the Hermes Stack) are instant card checkout through Stripe — buy and you get access right away. For custom Website Builds and Command Centers, payment is arranged after a short scoping call.
Do you have a referral program?
Yes! Refer someone to any of my services and when they sign up and pay, you get $100 back. No limit on how many people you can refer. Just have them mention your name when they book their call.
