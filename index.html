Frequently Asked Questions

No jargon. Just straight answers.

Do I need to be technical?
Not at all. I handle everything technical. If you can type a message, you can use your AI assistant. Think of it like texting a very smart coworker.
What kind of computer do I need?
Any modern Mac, Windows, or Linux computer works. If you're on Windows, I'll set up everything needed (it's free and built into Windows). I'll make sure your system is ready during our call.
What if I don't like it?
You get a 14-day money-back guarantee on the AI Kickstart and Full AI Setup. For the AI Command Center, return the Mac Mini in original condition for a full refund. No questions asked.
Is my data safe?
Your AI runs on your own computer. Your files, notes, and documents stay local. When the AI processes a request, only that specific prompt goes to the AI provider — your files aren't stored on their servers. It's significantly more private than uploading everything to a cloud service.
What tools can it connect to?
Email (Gmail, Outlook), Calendar (Google, Outlook), Social Media (LinkedIn, Instagram, X), Files, Notes (Notion), Business Tools (Slack, Stripe), and more. If it has a way to connect, we can probably hook it up.
Are there any ongoing costs?
My setup fee is one-time. The AI model itself uses a small pay-as-you-go fee — typically $10–$30/month depending on how much you use it, paid directly to the AI provider. I walk you through this during setup so there are no surprises.
What's the difference between the three packages?
AI Kickstart gets you up and running fast with the essentials. Full AI Setup adds social media integrations, custom automations, and more training time — it's the most popular choice. AI Command Center includes a dedicated Mac Mini that runs your AI 24/7 without touching your main computer — you just plug it in.
Can we do this in person?
Yes! I offer in-person setup for local clients. We can also do everything remotely over screenshare if that's easier for you.
How do I pay?
I accept Venmo, CashApp, and card payments. Payment is due at the start of the setup session, after our free consultation. No payment required for the initial call.
